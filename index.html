I am currently a tenure-track assistant professor in the SCIT(Computer Science) , Great Bay University since 2024.09. I received my M.S and Ph.D. degrees from the Department of Computer and Information Science, FST, University of Macau, under the supervision of Prof. Chi-Man Pun in 2018 and 2021 respectively. I received my B.Sc degree in Computer Science from Xidian University. My main research interests include Visual Computing, Computational Photography, with the intersection of Computer Vision and Graphics.
I am building Geneartive & intelligent Visual Computing Lab (GVC Lab), a new research lab at Great Bay University. Our lab studies Visual Computing, with the goal of building intelligent machines that for creators and ordinary users.
Several positions are available 🔥 🔥, please drop me an Email if you are interested.
